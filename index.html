Business Data Integration

Data integration and related practices add value to data, which in turn increases the value of the business processes that use the data.

Using a combination of Microsoft SSIS & Microsoft SQL Server procedures, we take data residing in different sources and combine them into one system. This allows our users to have a single point of view of their data.

Our solutions assist with effective decision making depending on aggregated, calculated, and time-series data values in a data warehouse—data and data structures derived from data integration. Our business practices such as operational BI or just-in-time inventory provides a data integration solution that can operate in real time or close to it (depending on the client data structures).

Business Data Reporting
